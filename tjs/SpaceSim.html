Please use a browser that supports "canvas"
  • Ship controls
  • ------------
  • w - move forward
  • a - rotate left
  • d - rotate right
  • s, e - pull up, down
  • B - switch to camera view
  • Camera controls
  • ---------------
  • w, a, s, d - move forward, left, back, right
  • r, f - move up, down
  • i, j, k, l - look up, left, down, right
  • I, J, K, L - orbit down, right, up, left
  • O - face origin
  • p - put camera at origin
  • W - decrease fov
  • S - increase fov
  • B - switch to ship view
